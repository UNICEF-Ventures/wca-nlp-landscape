# Akan

ISO 639-3: aka
Countries: Benin, Ghana

## Observations

- Major language in Ghana (Akan macrolanguage includes Twi and Fante)
- Ghana has active UNICEF NLP work

## Data

- **GhanaNLP Parallel Corpora** — Includes 5,001 Fante-English sentence pairs (Fante is an Akan variety). Multi-domain, dialect-aware (Coastal vs Inland Fante). CC BY-NC-SA 4.0. Datasets on HuggingFace but not tagged with language IDs: https://huggingface.co/collections/Ghana-NLP/data-collection-project
