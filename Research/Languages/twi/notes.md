# Twi

ISO 639-3: twi
Countries: Ghana

## Observations

- **Use Cases Document**: Ghana AGOO platform priority language (most widely spoken, high coverage across southern Ghana)
- Ghana has active UNICEF NLP work

## Data

- **GhanaNLP Parallel Corpora** — 14,875 human-translated Twi-English sentence pairs (largest in collection). Multi-domain, dialect-aware (Asante vs Akuapem Twi), tonal annotations. CC BY-NC-SA 4.0. Datasets on HuggingFace but not tagged with language IDs: https://huggingface.co/collections/Ghana-NLP/data-collection-project
